Data Science

You will benefit from a curriculum integrated with real-world experience, and kickstart your data science journey in a school that is known for its strength and expertise in data science and data analytics. With ICT’s strong relationships with the industry, collaborations such as industry talks and field trips provide students with practical data science experience.

In your first year, you will focus on building core computing skills in programming, databases and use of analytics. Besides understanding data science fundamentals and how it affects the competitiveness of organisations, you will also learn statistical concepts, as well as techniques for creating effective visualisations for analyses and presentations.

In your second year, you will deepen your knowledge in the area of data analytics. You will learn how to prepare data for analysis and modelling purposes, and how to extract valuable data from multiple sources and propose scalable solutions. You’ll also get a chance to apply agile development in an enterprise-critical data operations pipeline.

In your third year, you will embark on the Industry Kickstart Programme, which enhances your readiness for the workplace. You will explore emerging trends, apply machine learning techniques and embark on data science projects from industry to experience end-to-end Data Science workflows. This integration of knowledge and skills together with an internship will put you in good stead for work in this fast-growing sector.

By the time you graduate from ICT, you will have been exposed to many reputable enterprise platforms such as Microsoft Azure and SAP etc.
